The Work

What Subtitling & Captioning Covers

From a single YouTube video to a full series of corporate training clips - accurate, timed subtitles delivered in the format your platform or workflow requires.

SRT & VTT Subtitle Files

Standard subtitle files ready to upload directly to YouTube, Vimeo, LinkedIn, or any platform that accepts external captions. No re-encoding of your video required.

Burned-In Subtitles

Subtitles permanently embedded into the video file - ideal for social media clips, presentations, or any context where a separate file isn't practical. Delivered as a ready-to-share video.

Multilingual Subtitles

The same video subtitled into a different language - German content made accessible to English-speaking audiences, or English videos reaching German, Croatian, or other speakers. 90+ languages available.

Closed Captions for Accessibility

Captions that include speaker identification and non-speech sounds - suited for accessibility compliance, deaf and hard-of-hearing viewers and platforms with accessibility requirements.

Corporate & Training Video Captions

Internal training videos, onboarding content and recorded presentations captioned for staff - especially useful for multilingual teams where not everyone shares the same working language.

Batch Video Processing

Multiple videos processed consistently - same timing standards, same formatting, same quality throughout. Useful for podcast video series, course content, or event recordings.

Who It's For

Content Creators & YouTubers

The Output

What You Get Back

Accurate, timed subtitles in the format you need - ready to upload, share, or embed without any further editing on your end.

You Receive
  • SRT or VTT subtitle file for direct platform upload
  • Burned-in video file if hardcoded subtitles are requested
  • English subtitles manually reviewed to native standard (IELTS C1)
  • Accurate timing - no subtitle drift on standard-length videos
  • Inaudible or unclear sections flagged rather than guessed
  • Delivered via email or file-sharing link
How It Works
  • Send your video file or share a YouTube / Vimeo link
  • Confirm the language, format and any style preferences
  • Standard turnaround: 24 hours per hour of video
  • Rush delivery available - ask about availability
  • Revisions included for timing or content corrections
