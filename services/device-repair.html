The Work

What We Fix

Every job starts with a full diagnostic - no guessing, no unnecessary parts. We tell you exactly what's wrong before any work begins.

Full Hardware Diagnostics

Systematic board-level testing of motherboard, CPU, RAM, GPU, power circuitry and thermal systems. We isolate faults precisely - no parts replaced unnecessarily.

Screen Replacement

Cracked, dead, flickering, or backlight-failed displays replaced across all major laptop brands. Original-spec panels sourced for like-for-like quality.

Battery & Charging Port

Battery not holding charge, or charging port damaged? We replace both with quality parts and test under load before return.

RAM & Storage Upgrades

Slow machine? Upgrade RAM or replace your spinning hard drive with a fast SSD. Significant performance improvements for most laptops over 3 years old.

Data Recovery

Files lost to drive failure, accidental formatting, or OS corruption recovered wherever technically possible. We handle cases others turn away.

OS & Software Setup

Windows, Linux and macOS installation, driver setup, security configuration and performance tuning. Clean installs or in-place recovery - your choice.
